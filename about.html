About Me



Daniel Cullen

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of California Santa Barbara Department of Economics. I completed my undergraduate degree at the University of Florida and my Masters degree at University of Colorado Denver. My current research explores econometric methods when working with spatial or clustered data.

My non-professional interests include trail running, bouldering, and beach volleyball. You can find me on Strava at dcullen07 and view my Santa Barbara running heatmap below.
